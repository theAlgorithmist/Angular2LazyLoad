Three Stooges Episodes

The Stooges starred in a large number of shorts, and even some films. The lure of The Three Stooges proved to be timeless with the film remake, 'The Three Stooges, The Movie' in 2012. This section provides an abbreviated list of classic films and episodes that starred anywhere from one to three of The Stooges. In one short, 'Hold That Lion', Moe, Larry, Curly, and Shemp all appeared in the same episode.
